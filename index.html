Resume
Summary

I am a control systems and robotics engineer with experience developing software for both aerospace and wearable robotics applications. My software experience coupled with my Mechanical Engineering degree allows me to offer a rare combination of math and physics intuition alongside the practical know how required for implementing control algorithms on embedded systems.

Significant achievements include a mathematical formulation for quaternion based multibody dynamics, a non-linear feedback linearization controller for a satellite, and the design of an impedance controller for an open-source robotic leg. Other experience includes kalman filters, BLDC motor modeling, and general actuator and sensor selection.

Research interests: non-linear and digital control systems, bio-mechatronics, and trajectory generation
