Summary

I am a robotics and controls software engineer with a master's degree in mechanical engineering and experience developing software for defense, aerospace, and wearable robotics applications. I specialize in motion control, state estimation, and dynamics simulation, while also bringing broad experience in robotic software infrastructureâ€”including containerization, build systems, Linux, and ROS2.

This combination of academic background and real-world software experience allows me to offer a rare combination of math and physics intuition alongside the practical skills needed to implement control algorithms on embedded systems.

Notable work includes implementing an Unscented Kalman Filter for 3D vehicle localization, designing a nonlinear trajectory-tracking controller for satellite attitude control, and developing an impedance controller for an open-source robotic leg. Additional experience includes BLDC motor modeling and general actuator and sensor selection.

Research interests: autonomous vehicles, non-linear and digital control systems, bio-mechatronics, and trajectory generation
